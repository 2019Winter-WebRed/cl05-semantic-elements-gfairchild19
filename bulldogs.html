English Bulldogs are the Best!

All the information you need to know about English Bulldogs and why they make great furry friends!

The English Bulldog is a small to medium sized animal that is dependable, very sweet, and only needs moderate excercise!

They are very laid back and low tolerant animals. They rarely ever bark and don't always need lots of attention. However, they do drool often and snore very loudly, so I wouldn't recommend letting them sleep in the bed.

They are absolutely great with kids. They don't need much excercise, a brief walk and nap on the couch is just about it. They are also the least picky eaters and will eat pretty much anything they are given.
